1. Resolution

Prove the following using resolution. (Negate the conclusion, convert all formulas to CNF and then try to prove a contradiction.)

用分辨率证明以下。（否定结论，将所有公式转换为 CNF，然后尝试证明一个矛盾。）

  1. Given KB ={ P∧Q }, proof that KB |= P∨Q.
  2. Given KB ={ P∨Q, Q⇒(R∧S), (P∨R)⇒U }, proof that KB |= U.

Answer:

  1. Negate conclusion: ¬P∧¬Q.

    Total of four sentences: P, Q, ¬P, ¬Q.

    Resolve P with ¬P and Q with ¬Q gives {}.

    This means we have found a contradiction, or KB |= P∨Q is true.

  2. Negate conclusion: ¬U.

    Convert to CNF: KB ={P∨Q,(¬Q∨R)∧(¬Q∨S) ,(¬P∨U)∧(¬R∨U)}

    Total of six sentences.

    Resolve ¬P∨U with ¬U which gives¬P. ¬R∨U with ¬U gives ¬R.

    ¬P∨Q and ¬P resolves in Q.

    Q and ¬Q∨R resolves in R which contradicts with ¬R from the first resolution. Thus, KB|=U is true.

2. Problem: Hooligans

  1. Express the following sentences in first order logic and put them in CNF:

    • Peter has a season ticket for Ipswich Town Football Club.

      彼得有一张伊普斯维奇城足球俱乐部的季票。

      • Ticket(Peter)

    • Every Ipswich Town season ticket holder is a loyal supporter.

      每个伊普斯维奇镇季票持有者都是忠实的支持者。

      • ∀x, Ticket(x) ⇒ Loyal(x)
      • ¬Ticket(x)∨Loyal(x)

    • At a fiercely contested match two seasons ago, seats were thrown on to the pitch at the end of the match.

      在两个赛季前一场激烈的比赛中，比赛结束时座位被扔到了球场上。

      • ∃x, Throw(x)
      • Throw(x0)

    • Loyal supporters do not throw items on to the pitch.

      忠诚的支持者不会将物品扔到球场上。

      • ∀x, Loyal(x) ⇒ ¬Throw(x)
      • ¬Loyal(x) ∨ ¬Throw(x)

  2. Prove that Peter did not throw a seat onto the pitch using resolution.

    证明彼得没有使用决议将座位扔到球场上。

    Draw the proof tree, clearly identifying the substitutions you make at each step of the proof.

    • We want to prove ¬Throw(Peter).

      We take the negation Throw(Peter), then the proof goes:

      • Ticket(Peter),¬Ticket(x)∨Loyal(x): Loyal(Peter)(Peter/x)
      • Loyal(Peter),¬Loyal(x)∨¬Throw(x): ¬Throw(Peter)(Peter/x)
      • ¬Throw(Peter), Throw(Peter): ∅

3. Problem: Knights and knaves

On the island of Knights and Knaves, everything a Knight says is true and everything a Knave says is false. You meet two people, Alice and Bob:

在Knights and Knaves岛上，骑士说的都是真的，而无赖说的都是假的。你遇到了两个人，爱丽丝和鲍勃：

  • Alice 说：“Bob 和我都不是 Knaves。”
  • Bob说：“Alice是个knaves”。

Using the proposition A to represent “Alice is a Knight” (so¬A means “Alice is a Knave”) and B to represent “Bob is a Knight”:

  1. Formulate what Alice and Bob said

    表述 Alice 和 Bob 所说的话

    • Alice: A∧B
    • Bob: ¬A

  2. Formulate that what they said is true if and only if they are knights

    表述他们所说的是真的当且仅当他们是骑士

    • Alice: A⇔A∧B
    • Bob: B⇔¬A

  3. Put those into CNF form

    把它们变成 CNF 形式

    • A⇔A∧B
    • A⇒A∧B, A∧B⇒A
    • ¬A∨(A∧B),¬(A∧B)∨A
    • ¬A∨A,(tautology useless for proof),¬A∨B,¬A∨A∨B (tautology)
    • B⇔¬A
    • B⇒¬A,¬A⇒B
    • ¬B∨¬A, A∨B

  4. Use resolution to prove who is what.

    用分辨率来证明谁是什么

    • ¬A∨B, A∨B:B (proof that Bob is a Knight)
    • B,¬B∨¬A:¬A (proof that Alice is a Knave)

If you liked that problem, try searching online for the “Hardest Logic Puzzle Ever”.

4. Problem: Unification

For each of the following pairs of atomic sentences, give the most general unifier, if it exists.

对于以下每对原子句子，请给出最一般的统一词（如果存在）。

  1. P(A, B, B), P(x, y, z)
  2. Q(y, G(A, B)), Q(G(x, x), y)
  3. Older(Father(y), y), Older(Father(x), John)
  4. Knows(Father(y), y), Knows(x, x)

5. Problem: Genealogy

A popular children’s riddle is “Brothers and sisters I have none, but that man’s father is my father’s son. What is his link to me?”

一个流行的儿童谜语是“兄弟姐妹我没有，但那个男人的父亲是我父亲的儿子。他跟我有什么联系？”

To solve this problem we will use a language containing a binary predicate Brother, a binary predicate Son, a unary function Father and the constant Me.

为了解决这个问题，我们将使用一种包含二元谓词 Brother、二元谓词 Son、一元函数 Father 和常量 Me 的语言。

  1. Formulate the quoted sentences in first order logic.

    以一阶逻辑制定引用的句子。

    What is the goal of the problem?

    问题的目标是什么？

    • ∀x,¬Brother(x, M e)
    • Son(Father(X0), Father(M e))

  2. To be able to solve the problem you also need to formulate how the different family links interact

    为了能够解决问题，您还需要制定不同的家庭链接如何相互作用

    • Brother is irreflexive (no one is his own brother) and symmetric(if x is the brother of y, then y is the brother of x)

      • ∀x,¬Brother(x, x)
      • ∀x, y, Brother(x, y)⇒Brother(y, x)

    • Father and Son are inverse family links (requires 2 sentences)

      • ∀x, Son(x, Father(x))
      • ∀x, y, Son(x, y)⇒y=Father(x) (needs the = symbol)

    • Brothers have the same father.

      What of the reciprocal?

      • ∀x, y, Brother(x, y)⇒Father(x) =Father(y)
      • The reciprocal is not true in general: two persons with the same father are brothers only if they are different.
      • ∀Father(x) =Father(y)⇒Brother(x, y) ∨ x=y

  3. You will also need the = binary predicate.

    您还需要 = 二元谓词。

    The equality is a reflexive, symmetric transitive relation with the following property: if two terms are equal, then any function or predicate applied to both of them gives the same value. Formulate these properties for the genealogy language .

    等式是具有以下性质的自反对称传递关系：如果两个项相等，则应用于它们的任何函数或谓词都给出相同的值。为系谱语言制定这些属性。

    • ∀x, x=x
    • ∀x, y, x=y⇒y=x
    • ∀x, y, z, x=y∧y=z⇒x=z
    • ∀x, y, x=y⇒Father(x) =Father(y)
    • ∀x, y, z, x=y⇒Brother(x, z)⇒Brother(y, z)
    • ∀x, y, z, x=y⇒Brother(z, x)⇒Brother(z, y)
    • ∀x, y, z, x=y⇒Son(x, z)⇒Son(x, z)
    • ∀x, y, z, x=y⇒Son(z, x)⇒Son(z, y)

  4. Put those sentences into CNF and solve the problem by resolution

    把这些句子放到CNF中，通过解析解决问题

    • One can prove that X0 is my son.
