Quantitative imaging

Used definitions

The extraction of quantifiable features from medical images for the assessment of normal or the severity, degree of change, or status of a disease, injury, or chronic condition relative to normal

从医学图像中提取可量化的特征，以评估正常情况或疾病、损伤或慢性病相对于正常情况的严重程度、变化程度或状态

In the clinic, it may cover

  • Anatomical, functional and molecular imaging

    解剖、功能和分子成像

  • Data analyses

    数据分析

  • Display methods

    显示方式

  • Reporting structures

    报告结构

Biomarkers 生物标志物

Definition:

  • Also biological marker

  • A measurable indicator of some biological state or condition. Biomarkers are often measured and evaluated to examine normal biological processes, pathogenic processes, or pharmacologic responses to a therapeutic intervention

    某种生物状态或状况的可测量指标。 通常测量和评估生物标志物以检查正常的生物过程、致病过程或对治疗干预的药理反应。

An imaging biomarker: A biomarker detectable in an image

成像生物标志物：可在图像中检测到的生物标志物

Reproducible Research Recommendations

  • Reproducibility 再现性
    • A study is reproducible if you can take the original data and the computer code used to analyse the data and reproduce all of the numerical findings from the study. This may initially sound like a trivial task but experience has shown that it’s not always easy to achieve this seemingly minimal standard.
  • Replicability 可复制性
    • This is the act of repeating an entire study, independently of the original investigator without the use of original data （but generally using the same methods）.

Other definitions

Repeatability, Reproducibility, and Replicability: Tackling the 3R challenge in bio-interface science and engineering

可重复性、再现性和可复制性：应对生物界面科学与工程中的 3R 挑战

What does quantitative imply?

  • Reproducible 可重现

    • Agreement （up to a tolerance） of repeated measurements on the same patient

      对同一患者重复测量的一致性（达到公差）

  • Comparable 可比

    • Measures are suited to be compared between patients

      措施适合在患者之间进行比较

  • In practice also, Replicability 可复制性

    • Being able to independently achieve similar measurements across institutes, with slightly varying set-up, procedure, etc

      能够在不同机构独立实现类似的测量，设置、程序等略有不同

How to obtain quantitative measurements

  • Morphology 形态学

    • Thickness, length, surface, volume, shape, … of a structure

      The size of the detected object depends on it's intensity

  • Intensity 强度

    • Mean, median, standard deviation, skew, … of a structure

      The intensity parameters depend on the region considered

    Main features to measure

  • Texture 质地

    • A systematic local variation of the image values – Scale dependent.

      图像值的系统性局部变化——与比例相关。

    • Different types of texture are visible at different scales

      不同的尺度上可以看到不同类型的纹理

      Is in inherently a variation in the intensity 本质上是强度的变化

  • Frequencies 频率

    • Fourier, wavelet or other transforms

      Closely related to texture, and intensity

      与质地和强度密切相关

Relation between measures

  • All measures rely on a segmentation of the region of interest （ROI）

    所有措施都依赖于感兴趣区域的分割（ROI）

    • Which was performed using a region-based or edge-based technique

      这是使用基于区域或基于边缘的技术执行的

    • All measures are therefore influenced by the quality of the intensities or the intensity differences （edge）…

      因此，所有测量都受到强度质量或强度差异（边缘）的影响……

  • There is an overlap between texture and frequency

    纹理和频率之间存在重叠

    • But the are not the same 但不一样

  • Measures are related, but do carry complementary information

    措施是相关的，但确实带有补充信息

  • All require the imaging to be performed / adhere to certain standards

    所有都需要执行成像/遵守某些标准

    • In order to be quantitative

Quantitative measures in CT

  • Image intensity in CT is standardized CT中的图像强度是标准化的

    Remember: CT or Hounsfield units

    • Linear transformation of the original linear attenuation coefficient w

      原始线性衰减系数 w 的线性变换

    • HU value is linearly related to attenuation µ within that volume （~electron density）

      HU 值与该体积内的衰减 μ 线性相关（~电子密度） \[ CT(x, y) =1000 \frac{µ(x, y) − µ_{water}}{µ_{water}} \]

  • Intensity measurements are, to a certain extent, reproducible, comparable, replicable

    强度测量在某种程度上是可重复的、可比较的、可复制的

    • Quantification can be improved by standardizing the protocol （energy （kV）, exposure （mA）, reconstruction）

      量化可以通过标准化协议来提高（能量（kV），曝光（mA），重建）

CT has excellent geometric accuracy

CT具有出色的几何精度

  • Low geometric distortion 低几何失真

    • Distances, angles and straight lines are preserved

      保留距离、角度和直线

  • High resolution 高分辨率

    • Sub-millimetre resolution in all planes

      所有平面的亚毫米分辨率

Morphological assessments are, to a certain extent, reproducible, comparable, replicable

形态学评估在一定程度上是可重复的、可比较的、可复制的

  • Measurements related to the metabolic activity the cells

Standardized Uptake Value （SUV）

Standardise the measurement procedure

  • Always same activity injected
  • Always same time after injection
  • Always same account the size of the patient… euh?!?

Not a very practical way to work

  • Reproducible
  • Comparable on a patient basis, not interpatient
  • Difficult to replicate

\[ SUV(time)=\frac{Tracer\ uptake}{Injected\ Activity\ /\ Weight} \]

Under certain circumstances, 18FDG SUV correlates with metabolic rate of glucose and/or the number of viable tumor cells

在某些情况下，18FDG SUV 与葡萄糖代谢率和/或存活肿瘤细胞的数量相关

  • SUV is a simplified quantitative measure that can be routinely performed in clinical PET studies

    SUV 是一种简化的定量测量，可在临床 PET 研究中常规进行

Adjusts image intensities for 调整图像强度

  • Differences in patient size 患者大小的差异
  • Injected activity 注入活动
  • Correct activity to decay at a reference time 在参考时间衰减的正确活动

The resulting intensities correlate with metabolic activity of tumor cells AND are reproducible, comparable and replicable to a certain extent.

由此产生的强度与肿瘤细胞的代谢活动相关，并且在一定程度上是可重复的、可比较的和可复制的。

SUV Units

  • Based on the following assumptions （approximations）:

    基于以下假设（近似值）

    • Water-equivalent tissue 水当量组织

    • A body weight correction in grams 以克为单位的体重校正

    • Decay-correction to the time of injection 对注射时间的衰减校正

    • Known concentration in consistent units of mCi/ml or MBq/cc

      已知浓度，单位为 mCi/ml 或 MBq/cc

  • The SUV is a relative, unit-less quantity 一个相对的、无单位的数量

    • It relates the image derived radioactivity concentration to the whole-body concentration of the injected radioactivity

      它将图像衍生的放射性浓度与注射放射性的全身浓度相关联

    • Under standard settings （time, injection, activity, etc ）

      在标准设置下（时间、注射、活动等）

SUV 计算的变化

  • Instead of body mass 代替体重

    • Body surface area or lean body weight 体表面积或瘦体重
    • Sometimes denoted with subscripts （SUVBW, SUVBSA, SUVLBM）

  • Linear correction for circulating glucose 循环葡萄糖的线性校正

  • Designated uptake period 指定吸收期

    • Delayed scanning may reduce background physiologic uptake

      延迟扫描可能会减少背景生理摄取

Intensity-based features 基于强度的特征

  • Examples of intensity features over region Ω in image f(x) with N pixels

    • Mean intensity value \[ \mu = \frac{1}{N}\sum_{x∈\Omega}f(x) \]

    • Median, minimum and maximum values

    • Standard deviation \[ \sigma=\sqrt{\frac{1}{N}\sum_{x∈\Omega}(f(x)-\mu)^2} \]

    • Energy \[ E=\sum_{x∈\Omega}(f(x)+c)^2 \]

      • c is a constant such that all values within the brackets are positive
      • Energy is related to the content of intensities, irrespective of conventions such as HU

  • Derived from the histogram

    • Skewness

      • Measure of asymmetry \[ S_{sk}=\frac{\frac{1}{N}\sum_{x∈\Omega}(f(x)-\mu)^3}{\sigma^3} \]

    • Kurtosis

      • Measure of relative weight of the tails \[ S_{ku}=\frac{\frac{1}{N}\sum_{x∈\Omega}(f(x)-\mu)^4}{\sigma^4} \]

Shape

  • Examples of shape features over region Ω in image f(x) with N pixels

    • Volume

      • \[ v = \frac{1}{N}\sum_{x∈\Omega}v(x) \]

      • \[ v(x)=s_xs_ys_z \]

        the volume of a voxel

    • Largest diameter

      • Di the axes of the enclosing ellipsoid

      • \[ D_M=\max[D_1,D_2,D_3] \]

    • Smallest diameter

      • \[ d_m=\min[D_1,D_2,D_3] \]

    • Elongation

      • \[ \frac{D_M}{D_m} \]

  • Examples considering the area A of the surface of the structure

    • Relation to the surface of the compassing spheres

    • Surface Volume Ratio

      • \[ R_{SV}=\frac{S}{V} \]

    • Compactness

      • \[ C=\frac{V}{\sqrt{A^3\pi}} \]

Gray-level co-occurrence matrix 灰度共生矩阵 （GLCM）

  • The distribution of the number of occurrences of a pair of gray values and separated by a distance vector Δ = [Δx, Δy]

    由距离向量Δ = [Δx, Δy]分隔的一对灰度值的出现次数分布

\[ C\Delta_x,\Delta_y(i,j)=\sum^n_{x=1}\sum^m_{y=1}\left\{ \begin{aligned} 1 & & if\ I(x,y)=i\ and\ I(x+\Delta x,y+\Delta y)=j \\ 0 & &otherwise \end{aligned} \right. \]

      Too many negatives, a typical problem in CAD

  • Data balancing methods

    • Downsample your negatives
      • Select representative samples, e.g. SOM
    • Upsample your positives
      • Generate similar / plausible samples in feature space

Classification
  • Support vector machine
    • Using a radial basis function （RBF）
    • Using a soft margin
  • Tuning the parameters
    • 10-fold cross-validation on the training set

      Radiomics为处理高通量提取的定量特征其导致图像转化成可采数据，并且这些数据的随后的分析的决策支持

  • Process

    • Extract a lot of （quantitative） features 提取大量（定量）特征

      尝试对治疗的未来结果做出预测

Summary

Quantitative Imaging

  • The extraction of quantifiable features from medical images for the assessment of normal or the severity, degree of change, or status of a disease, injury, or chronic condition relative to normal

    从医学图像中提取可量化的特征，以评估正常情况或疾病、损伤或慢性病相对于正常情况的严重程度、变化程度或状态

  • In this context, quantitative implies reproducible, comparable, replicable

    在这种情况下，定量意味着可重复、可比较、可复制

  • Obtaining quantitative measures puts requirements on the image acquisition, and the image analysis / processing

    获得定量测量对图像采集和图像分析/处理提出了要求

  • In CT, quantitative measures can be obtained on intensity and morphology

    在 CT 中，可以获得强度和形态的定量测量

  • In MRI, quantitative measures can be obtained on morphology. Intensities can not be directly compared. Specific MRI sequences or analyses, lead to physical quantities, which can be compared

    在 MRI 中，可以获得对形态学的定量测量。强度不能直接比较。特定的 MRI 序列或分析，导致可以比较的物理量

  • In PET, quantitative measurements of intensity can be obtained using standardized uptake values （SUV）

    在 PET 中，可以使用标准化摄取值（SUV）获得强度的定量测量

  • To reliably extract quantitative measures from images, there is a need for standardization and clear criteria

    为了从图像中可靠地提取定量测量，需要标准化和明确的标准

    • QIBA alliance for standard measurement of imaging biomarkers

      QIBA 成像生物标志物标准测量联盟

    • RECIST or WHO response criteria in oncology

      RECIST 或 WHO 肿瘤学反应标准

Feature extraction

  • The general pipeline involves
    • the acquisition of images
    • Pre-processing and segmentation of the region of interest
    • Extraction of the quantitative measure
    • Diagnostic assessment based on the measure
  • Intensity features cover
    • The mean and standard deviation
    • The median
    • The energy
    • The skewness and kurtosis
    • …
  • Shape features cover
    • The volume
    • The elongation
    • The compactness
    • …
  • Another group of features is related to texture （not covered in depth here）

Computer-aided diagnosis

  • The general pipeline involves
    • The acquisition of images
    • Pre-processing
    • Detection of abnormalities and segmentation
    • Extraction of many quantitative image features
    • Classification of the abnormality
  • Classification is a supervised machine learning technique
    • Based on a training dataset, learn an optimal decision boundary between classes
    • Given a new data point, decide to which class the point belongs
  • Example of a full pipeline for a CAD problem for lung nodules
    • Pre-processing: images are resampled, lungs are segmented
    • Candidate detection: detect suspicious structures
    • Reduce the amount of false positives using feature extraction and classification
      • First train a classifier based on the features of a training dataset
      • Then apply the classifier based on the feature of the input structure

Radiomics

  • The general pipeline involves
    • The acquisition of images
    • Segmentation of tumours
    • Extraction of many quantitative image features
    • Classification based on clinical endpoints （survival, gene expression, etc.）
  • Radiomic signatures are extracted from the tumor
    • Including intensity statistics
    • Shape features
    • Texture features
    • Wavelet features
  • Signatures were shown to correlated with the outcome of the patients （survival）

Question

Describe how to obtain quantitative measurements from the following modalities. Discuss how each can be used to obtain morphological and intensity measurements

  1. Computed tomography

    • Morphology:

      CT has excellent geometric accuracy 具有出色的几何精度

      • Low geometric distortion 低几何失真

        • Distances, angles and straight lines are well preserved

          距离、角度和直线都保存完好

      • High resolution 高分辨率

        • Sub-millimetre resolution in all planes is possible

          所有平面的亚毫米分辨率是可能的

      • Morphological assessments are, to a certain extent, reproducible, comparable, replicable

        在某种程度上，形态学评估是可重复的、可比较的、可复制的

    • Intensity:

      Image intensity in CT is standardized because of the use of CT or Hounsfield unit

      由于使用 CT 或 Hounsfield 单位，CT 中的图像强度是标准化的

      • HU value is linearly related to attenuation 𝜇 within that volume （~electron density）

        HU 值与该体积内的衰减𝜇 线性相关（~电子密度） \[ CT(x,y)=1000\frac{\mu(x,y)-\mu_{water}}{\mu_{water}} \]

      • Intensity measurements in CT are therefore are, to a certain extent, reproducible, comparable, replicable

        因此，CT 中的强度测量在某种程度上是可重复的、可比较的、可复制的

      • Quantification of intensities can be further improved by standardizing the protocol （energy （kV）, exposure （mA）, reconstruction）

        通过标准化协议（能量（kV）、暴露（mA）、重建）可以进一步提高强度的量化

  2. Magnetic resonance

    • Morphology:

      Geometric accuracy is usually lower than CT, depending on protocol

      几何精度通常低于 CT，具体取决于协议

      • Lower resolution, depending on the site and protocol

        较低的分辨率，取决于站点和协议

      • Some sequences exhibit distortion （DWI）

        一些序列表现出失真（DWI）

      Can still be used for morphological assessments, given a suitable tolerance

      仍可用于形态学评估，给定合适的耐受性

    • Intensities:

      Image intensities in MRI are relative

      MRI 中的图像强度是相对的

      • MRI is in essence a contrast map MRI本质上是一张对比图

      • No direct physical meaning of the image intensity in standard MRI

        标准 MRI 中图像强度没有直接的物理意义

    • Quantitative intensity measurements can be derived by extracting physical quantities using advanced MRI sequences.

      可以通过使用高级 MRI 序列提取物理量来导出定量强度测量值。

      Examples:

      • Perfusion measurements from dynamic contrast-enhanced （DCE） or perfusion weighted imaging (PWI)

        来自动态对比增强（DCE）或灌注加权成像（PWI）的灌注测量

        • Blood flow, blood volume, permeability, ...

      • Diffusion measurements from diffusion-weighted imaging （DWI）

        弥散加权成像（DWI）的弥散测量

      • Apparent diffusion coefficient （ADC）

        表观扩散系数（ADC）
