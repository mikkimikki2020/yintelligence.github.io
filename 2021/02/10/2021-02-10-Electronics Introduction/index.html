Technological point of view

Definition

The field of electronics refers to the study, design and use of systems that operate by controlling the flow of electrons (or other charge carriers) in active (semiconductor) and passive devices.

电子领域是指研究、设计和使用通过控制有源（半导体）和无源器件中的电子（或其他电荷载流子）流动来运行的系统。

  • Passive components 无源元件

    • Limited

    • dissipate energy 耗散能量

    • exhibit linear behaviours

      表现出线性行为

  • Active components 有源元件

    • 半导体 Semiconductors

    • amplify incoming signals. exhibit complex 放大输入信号

    • exhibit complex, non-linear behaviour

      表现出复杂的非线性行为

The difference between electricity/electrical circuits and electronics is the use of active components.

电力/电路和电子产品之间的区别在于使用了有源元件。

User’s point of view 用户视角

Most deal only with information: capturing, transmission, processing, representation, visualization, and storage of information or power.

大多数只处理信息：信息或权力的捕获、传输、处理、表示、可视化和存储。

Example: Strain gauge 应变片

A strain gauge is a type of electrical sensor. It’s primary use is to measure force or strain.

应变计是一种电子传感器。 它的主要用途是测量力或应变。

The resistance of a strain gauge changes when force is applied and this change will give a different electrical output. Strain gauges use this method to measure pressure, force, weight and tension.

当施加力时应变计的电阻会发生变化，这种变化会产生不同的电输出。 应变计使用这种方法来测量压力、力、重量和张力。

When external forces are applied to a stationary object there are two forces present; stress and strain.

当外力作用在静止物体上时，有两种力存在； 压力和应变。

  • Stress 压力

    • the resisting force of the object (like a push back)

      应力是物体的抵抗力（如推回）

  • Strain 应变

    • the displacement and deformation of the object and this is the force which can be measured by a strain gauge

      应变是物体的位移和变形，这是可以通过应变计测量的力

Because they are small and highly sensitive strain gauges can measure the contraction or expansion of an object even if it is just a small amount when they are correctly bonded to an object or device.

因为它们体积小且灵敏度高，应变计可以测量物体的收缩或膨胀，即使它们在正确粘合到物体或设备上时只是很小的量。

在许多测试应用中，应变计被用作预防措施。通常，当应变计给出特定读数时，将触发警报以通知用户已达到容量，这意味着可以在问题变得危险之前解决问题。

  • Stress on railway lines
  • Stresses on aircraft wing deflection
  • Aircraft component testing
  • Rotational strain on turbines, wheels, fans, propellers and motors
  • Testing ships hulls
  • Testing structural components for bridges and buildings
  • Automotive testing

Micro-electronics 微电子

“微电子学是电子学的一个子领域。微电子学，顾名思义，就是研究和制造非常小的电子元件。它是一种基于IC技术设计和实现系统和电路的技术，即实现 在 1 个半导体晶体中。”

用于基本构建块

  • 晶体管的最小尺寸的深亚微米级（10-5 nm）
  • IC 的典型尺寸为 1 cm x 1 cm。

Electronics: discrete components = building blocks for simple systems (specialized components: examples for implementation)

Micro-electronics: bigger building blocks on one chip (IC)= building blocks for complex systems

微电子：单芯片（IC）上更大的构建块 = 复杂系统的构建块

Why semiconductors? 40:48

Characteristics of both conductors (resistors) and isolators (capacitors)

导体（电阻器）和隔离器（电容器）的特性

Conductor characteristics 导体特性

  • Transport of charge carriers through (low) resistance

    通过（低）电阻传输电荷载流子

  • Information and signal transport

    信息和信号传输

  • Voltage → modulated conduction → information and signal processing

    电压 → 调制传导 → 信息和信号处理

Isolator characteristics 隔离器特性

  • Storage of charge carriers 电荷载体的存储

  • Controlled access to capacitors → controlled voltage → memory

    受控访问电容器 → 受控电压 → 内存

Electronics for computing 计算电子

在 2004 年之前，single threaded floating-point单线程浮点增长速度甚至超过整数性能，每年增长 64%：翻番周期为 73 周。 在那之后，它以每年 21% 的速度稳定下来。

Dennard Scaling 登纳德标度

Dennard scaling，也称为MOSFET scaling，是一种缩放定律，它粗略地指出，随着晶体管变小，它们的功率密度 Power density保持不变，因此功耗与面积成正比；电压和电流都随长度缩放（向下）。

  • Power = alpha × CFV 2

    • Alpha - percent time switched 百分比时间切换
    • C - capacitance 电容
    • F - frequency 频率
    • V - voltage 电压

  • Capacitance is related to area 电容与面积有关

    As the size of the transistors shrunk, and the voltage was reduced, circuits could operate at higher frequencies at the same power.

    随着晶体管尺寸的缩小和电压的降低，电路可以在相同功率下以更高的频率运行。

  • End of Dennard Scaling

    Dennard Scaling ignored the "leakage current" and "threshold voltage", which establish a baseline of power per transistor.

    Dennard Scaling 忽略了“泄漏电流”和“阈值电压”，它们建立了每个晶体管的功率基线。

    • As transistors get smaller, power density increases because these do not scale with size

      随着晶体管变得越来越小，功率密度会增加，因为它们不会随着尺寸而缩放

    • These created a "Power Wall" that limited practical processor frequency to around 4GHz since 2006

      自 2006 年以来，这些创造了一个“Power Wall”，将实际处理器频率限制在 4GHz 左右

Computing speed 计算速度

Computing speed does not scale linearly with the number of cores! 2 x 3 Ghz < 6Ghz

计算速度与内核数量不成线性关系！2 x 3 Ghz < 6Ghz

  • Coordination overhead, cache coherency

    协调开销，缓存一致性

  • Not all problems lend themselves to (much) parallelization

    并非所有问题都适合（很多）并行化

  • Programming difficulty (harder to reason about, complex dependencies)

    编程难度（更难推理，复杂的依赖关系）

Solutions: increased processor complexity 增加处理器的复杂性

Modern processors are not just “faster” clones of older processor generations

Processors have become much more complex, to the point that it has become nearly impossible for a single person to grasp the entire system.

处理器变得更加复杂，以至于一个人几乎不可能掌握整个系统。

  • x86-64 ASM (assembly) has essentially become another programming layer

    x86-64 ASM（汇编）本质上已经成为另一个编程层

  • High-performance analysis has become statistical

    高性能分析已成为统计

Some notable advances:

  • High core count (“AMD Threadripper” goes up to 64 cores as of 2020)

    高内核数（“AMD Threadripper”到 2020 年将达到 64 个内核）

  • Multi-threading within cores, context switching registers

    内核中的多线程、上下文切换寄存器

  • Write-combine on bus

    在总线上写入组合

  • Instruction-level parallelism (pipelining, superscalar)

    指令级并行（流水线、超标量）

  • Speculative execution (branch prediction)

    投机执行（分支预测）

  • Out-of-order execution

    乱序执行

  • Single instruction, multiple data (SIMD)

    单指令多数据 (SIMD

  • Advanced power management, even within chip regions

    先进的电源管理，即使在芯片区域内

  • Complex cache hierarchy

    复杂的缓存层次结构

Solutions: different hardware topologies 不同的硬件拓扑 58:52

GPU (Graphics Processing Units) -1:02:01

many tasks, especially involving signals, lend themselves to extreme parallelization (graphics, light transport, sound, weather systems, ...)

许多任务，尤其是涉及信号的任务，都适合极端并行化（图形、光传输、声音、天气系统……）

TPU (Tensor Processing Units) - 1:02:54

deep learning requires large amounts of multiply-adds for computing convolutions → specialized hardware.

深度学习需要大量的乘加来计算卷积 → 专用硬件

Future of computing 计算前景

As complexity increases, optimization becomes increasingly important!

随着复杂性的增加，优化变得越来越重要！

  • Performance = Saving energy

    性能 = 节能

  • Tight feedback between software and hardware design

    软件和硬件设计之间的紧密反馈

  • Tune algorithms to specific architecture (Big-O analysis alone won’t do)

    针对特定架构调整算法（仅靠 Big-O 分析是不行的）

  • New paradigms (quantum, neuromorphic, FPGA)

    新范式（量子、神经形态、FPGA）

  • Robustness and errors in algorithms (loss of determinism), Statistical analysis of run-time

    算法的鲁棒性和错误（确定性丢失），运行时的统计分析

New manufacturing technologies 新的制造技术

Extreme Ultra-Violet (EUV) Lithography 极紫外 (EUV) 光刻

Wafer-scale integration 晶圆级集成

Shrinking transistors reaches fundamental limits(1:22:48), Atomic-level manufacturing variations

缩小晶体管达到基本极限 (1:22:48)，原子级制造变化

As a metal oxide–semiconductor field effect transistor (MOSFET) shrinks, the gate dielectric (yellow) thickness approaches several atoms (0.5 nm at the 22-nm technology node).

随着金属氧化物半导体场效应晶体管 (MOSFET) 的缩小，栅极电介质（黄色）厚度接近几个原子（22 纳米技术节点为 0.5 纳米）

Heterogeneous computing at all scales 全尺度异构计算

Arrange GPU CPU together: At chip-level, node-level, cluster-level and network-level

将GPU CPU排列在一起：芯片级、节点级、集群级、网络级

Quantum computing 量子计算

  • Series of bits are encoded as quantum superpositions of 0 and 1, denoted qubits
  • Myth: quantum computers can perform calculations exponentially faster than classical computers.
  • Fact: they can solve specific problem classes highly efficiently, but not others. (Applications in cryptography, optimization, accurate physical simulations of quantum systems)
  • Prediction: quantum computers will become a specialized unit for certain problem classes, like the GPU. (QPU?)

Departure from von Neumann architecture

神经形态计算始于使用模拟电路来模拟大脑中的突触结构，大脑擅长通过噪音和学习确定模式， 神经形态 CPU 擅长处理离散、清晰的数据。
