The Diode 二极管 9:38

Chapter 3 The diode 15 ~ 28

The diode is the simplest semiconductor component, containing two leads. The diode is visualised with an arrow. This arrow visualises in which way the current can flow through the diode, since the diode only accepts one way of current flow.

二极管是最简单的半导体元件，包含两条引线lead。二极管用箭头表示。该箭头显示了电流可以通过哪种方式流过二极管，因为二极管只接受一种方式的电流流动。

Goal

  • use the diode as a switch in a rectifier circuit 使用二极管作为整流电路中的开关

How is a diode built 二极管是如何构建的?

Physically the diode is realised by the combination of two types of semiconductors

物理上二极管是通过两种半导体的组合来实现的

  • P-type semiconductor is one where the free carriers are positively charged

    P型半导体是一种自由载流子带正电的半导体

  • N-type semiconductors have negatively charged free carriers.

    N 型半导体具有带负电的自由载流子

  • Where the two types of semiconductor meet, is called the diode junction.

    两种类型的半导体相遇的地方称为二极管结

How does a diode work? 10:51 - 18:37

How does the applied voltage affect the current?

在上图中，正电压 V 施加在二极管上。 由于该电压，存在电场 E 使正负carrier彼此加速。

在junction处，这两种类型的free carriers相遇并cancel out through recombination。 在左侧二极管中流动的正电流是由从左向右移动的正载流子产生的。流出的正电流是因为从右向左移动的负载流子。 在这种情况下，二极管表现为正向偏置 forward bias,，以正确的箭头方向通过电流。

在上图中，在二极管上施加负电压 V。 这会产生一个电场，使负载流子和正载流子远离junction。 该junction现在充当insulator，几乎没有剩余的自由载流子。 该绝缘体具有高resistance，通过二极管的电流将非常低。 在这种情况下，二极管在反向偏置reverse bias中起作用。

The built-in potential in a semiconductor equals the potential across the depletion region in thermal equilibrium（半导体中的内建电势等于热平衡中耗尽区的电势）。

Since thermal equilibrium implies that the Fermi energy is constant throughout the p-n diode, the built-in potential equals the difference between the Fermi energies, EFn and EFp, divided by the electronic charge.

由于热平衡意味着整个PN二极管的费米能量是恒定的，因此内置电势等于费米能量EFn和EFp之差除以电子电荷。

It also equals the sum of the bulk potentials of each region, ϕn and ϕp, since the bulk potential quantifies the distance between the Fermi energy and the intrinsic energy.

它还等于每个区域的体势之和， ϕn和ϕp，因为体势量化了费米能量和本征能量之间的距离。

Characteristics of a diode 特性 18:40

Current-Voltage characteristic （I-V curve）

  • For positive voltages, the current is also positive and increases exponentially with the applied voltage.

    对于正电压，电流也是正的，并且随着施加的电压呈指数增加。

  • The current starts flowing when the voltage is above the threshold voltage Vd.

    当电压高于阈值电压Vd时，电流开始流动。

This threshold voltage is dependent on the used semiconductor, but since most diodes are made with a silicon semiconductor, the typical threshold voltage of a diode is the one for Si, Vd=0.7 V.

该阈值电压取决于所使用的半导体，但由于大多数二极管是用硅半导体制成的，二极管的典型阈值电压是 Si 的阈值电压，Vd=0.7 V。

  • For an applied voltage equal to zero, there will be no current flow in the diode.

    对于等于零的施加电压，二极管中将没有电流流动。

  • For negative voltages, there will be a small negative leakage current –IS, this is called the saturation current of the diode.

  • 对于负电压，会有一个小的负漏电流IS，这称为二极管的饱和电流

\[ I = I_{S}(e^{\frac{qV}{\eta k_{B}T}}-1) \]

  • q: the electron charge 电子电荷
  • k: the constant of Boltzmann 玻尔兹曼常数
  • T: absolute temperature 绝对温度
  • η: an empirical constant 经验常数经验常数 called the ideality factor 理想因子 that is dependent on the used semiconductor in the diode.

For semiconductors with a large bandgap （such as silicon） η=2.

For smaller bandgap semiconductors （like germanium） η=1

Application possibilities 应用可能性

From the diode characteristic we can define the application possibilities for a diode.

根据二极管特性，我们可以定义二极管的应用可能性。

  • Use the diode as a switch, it behaves as an element that conducts current completely （low resistance）, or doesn’t conduct current at all （very high resistance）

    使用二极管作为开关，它表现为完全传导电流（低电阻）或根本不传导电流（非常高电阻）的元件

  • In more complicated applications it can be used as a general nonlinear element

    在更复杂的应用中，它可以用作一般的非线性元件

The diode as a nonlinear element 作为非线性元件 23:00

The use of a diode as a general nonlinear element is visualised.

二极管作为一般非线性元件的使用是可视化的。

In this case the applied voltage over the diode is non-ideal, with a direct voltage U, an alternating voltage vw（t） and a non-zero load resistance R.

在这种情况下，二极管上施加的电压是非理想的，直流电压 U、交流电压vw（t）和非零负载电阻 R。

The voltage over the diode can be split up in an average time independent value and a time dependent signal component: \[ V_{D}(t)=V_{D} + v_{D}(t) \] The same happens with the current through the diode: \[ I_{D}(t)=I_{D} + i_{D}(t) \] The analysis of the circuit in happens in two steps.

  • DC analysis: the time independent average values of voltages and currents are calculated

    直流分析：计算电压和电流的时间无关平均值

  • Small signal model: a linearization is done for small variations around the DC point or bias point

  • 小信号模型：对直流点或偏置点周围的小变化进行线性化

DC Analysis 直流分析

For the DC analysis all the alternating components are set to zero.

对于 DC 分析，所有交流分量都设置为零。

This gives the simplified circuit:

The time independent values over the diode VD and ID have to fulfil two characteristics which are visualised.

  • The diode characteristic is blue 二极管特性为蓝色

  • In the external circuit, U and R are linear elements and thus they have a linear curve between VD and ID

    在外部电路中，U 和 R 是线性元件，因此它们在VD和ID之间具有线性曲线

  • This straight line in the VD, ID plane is called the resistive load line

    VD, ID平面中的这条直线称为电阻负载线

To draw this line, at least 2 points need to be known.

  1. The intersection of the resistive load line and the VD axis （this means ID=0） is VD=U.
    • For the first point an open circuit is made, this means that ID = 0
    • There is no current flow through R and VD = U.
  2. This is the intersection of the resistive load line and the current axis. If the two points are connected, we get the complete resistive load line （red）.
    • For the second point VD is put to zero （short circuit） in the external circuit.
    • The current through R will be ID=U/R.

The intersection between both characteristics （diode curve and resistive load line） gives the DC values of VD and ID of the voltage VD（t） and the current ID（t） in the circuit.

The coordinates （VD, ID） is called the bias point 偏置点, operating point or Q-point of the circuit.

AC equivalent model AC等效模型 / small signal model 小信号模型

For the calculation of the components in the circuit, the small signal model can be used as visualised.

For AC voltages that differ from zero in the circuit,VD（t） and ID（t） will fluctuate around the bias point 围绕偏置点波动.

The point （VD（t）, ID（t）） is an element of the diode curve （blue）. When the fluctuations are not too large around the bias point, the nonlinear diode curve in blue can be replaced by the tangent of this diode curve in the bias point. In this way we have a linear approximation of our nonlinear diode curve that works for small fluctuations around the bias point.

Conclusion: the signal components vD（t） and iD（t） of the diode behave as a resistance RD.

The diode is replaced by the dynamic resistance and all the DC components are removed from the circuit. The removal of the DC voltage source U （U=0） is equal to a short circuit. A capacitance is added for signals with high frequencies.

二极管被动态电阻取代，所有直流分量都从电路中去除。去除直流电压源 U （U=0） 就等于短路。为高频信号添加电容

Summary

A small summary of how a time dependent nonlinear circuit can be handled:

  • DC analysis: analysis of the time independent nonlinear circuit

    DC 分析：时间无关非线性电路的分析

  • AC analysis/small signal model: analysis of the time dependent linear circuit

    AC 分析/小信号模型：时间相关线性电路的分析

Introduction 40:16

A type of transistor that uses both electrons and holes as charge carriers.

一种同时使用电子和空穴作为电荷载流子的晶体管。

A BJT allows a small current injected at one of its terminals to control a much larger current flowing between two other terminals, making the device capable of amplification or switching.

BJT 允许在其一个端子处注入小电流，以控制在其他两个端子之间流动的大得多的电流，从而使器件能够放大或切换。

The first bipolar transistor was invented at Bell Labs by William Shockley, Walter Brattain, and John Bardeen in 1947. They were awarded the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1956 for the transistor.

Bipolar transistors are mostly used for amplification , power regulation, at small scale.

双极晶体管主要用于小规模放大，功率调节。

Basic structure & symbols 基本结构和符号 42:22

Like the diode is built from two semiconductor layers, so is the bipolar transistor build from three semiconductor layers. Two types of bipolar transistors exist, dependent on the used n-type or p-type semiconductors. This is visualised in Figure.

就像二极管由两个半导体层构成一样，双极晶体管也由三个半导体层构成。根据使用的 n 型或 p 型半导体，存在两种类型的双极晶体管。这在图中可视化。

The transistor has three external leads: the Collector, Emitter and Base.

晶体管具有三个外部引线：集电极、发射极和基极

  • The main current flows through the Collector and Emitter lead

    主电流流过集电极Collector和发射极Emitter引线

  • The direction of the arrow in the symbols of Figure indicate the normal current flow

    图中符号中的箭头方向表示正常电流

  • The amount of current through Collector and Emitter can be manipulated through a small current through the Base

    通过集电极和发射器的电流量可以通过通过基极Base的小电流来控制

The characteristics of the transistor can be described when two independent voltages and two independent currents are known.

当已知两个独立的电压和两个独立的电流时，可以描述晶体管的特性。

  • Independent currents
    1. Base current Ib
    2. Collector current Ic.
  • Independent voltages
    1. Base-Emitter voltage Vbe
    2. Emitter-Collector voltage Vce.

The current that flows out of the Emitter will be Ib + Ic according to the current law of Kirchhoff 基尔霍夫电路定律, and the Base-Collector voltage will be Vce-Vbe according to the voltage law of Kirchhoff

基尔霍夫电路定律:

  1. 所有进入某节点的电流的总和等于所有离开这节点的电流的总和。
  2. 沿着闭合回路所有器件两端的电势差（电压）的代数和等于零。

Detailed structure 详细结构 NPN & PNP 50:05

NPN transistor structures （not to scale 不按比例）

An NPN transistor is realised by a thin p-type semiconductor layer in between two n-type semiconductor layers

NPN 晶体管由两个 n 型半导体层之间的薄 p 型半导体层实现

  • The collector is lightly doped so that the collector-base junction will have a high breakdown voltage

    集电极是轻度掺杂的，因此集电极-基极结将具有高击穿电压

  • The collector also needs to be heavily doped to minimize ohmic losses for high currents.

    集电极还需要重掺杂，以最大限度地减少高电流的欧姆损耗

    • → contradiction → heavy doping at contact only 仅在接触处重掺杂

  • The collector current travels shorter through the player than the Base current

    集电极电流通过播放器的时间比基极电流短

    • → 𝐼𝐵 ≪ 𝐼C

  • The current voltage characteristic Ib=f（Vbe） will be the same as a diode I-V curve.

    • The Base region is the thin p-layer in between the two n-layers of the Emitter and Collector.
    • Due to this reason the Collector current travels shorter through the p-layer than the Base current.
    • Here out it can be proven that the ratio Ib/Ic is much smaller than one and almost constant.

PNP transistor structures

The PNP transistor is realised by joining two p-layers around one thin n-layer. The Base region is connected to the n-layer. The Collector and Emitter are each connected to one of two p-layers. The operation of a PNP transistor is likewise to an NPN transistor.

Between Emitter and Base a diode characteristic exist –Ib = f（-Vbe）. –Ib and –Ic are positive. The ratio （–Ib）/（– Ic） is smaller than one and almost constant.

Models of operation 操作模式 1:01:45

DC bias point and AC small signal model 1:05:54

The bipolar transistor is a nonlinear element due to the presence of the diodes in the characteristics.

由于在特性中存在二极管，双极晶体管是非线性元件。

The analysis for the nonlinear transistor can be handled in the same way as the analysis for the diode.

First, we look for the DC bias point, afterwards we perform an AC small signal model.

In a first step the DC components of the currents and voltages are being considered, as visualised in the middle. For this DC component the general nonlinear equivalent circuit is studied. From this DC components the DC bias point of the transistor is determined.

In the second step the time dependent AC components are being considered around the time independent bias point. If the AC variations vbe（t） and ib（t） are small enough, the nonlinear characteristics at the input, the diode, can be made linear around the bias point, this is called the linearization. This means that if vbe（t） and ib（t） are small, the diode can be replaced by an equivalent dynamic resistance as seen in equations.

This dynamic resistance of the diode is given by equation: Rd=25mV/Ib. The complete time dependent currents and voltages in the transistor are the sum of the DC bias point , from a nonlinear time independent analysis, and the AC small signal model, from a time dependent linear analysis.

The Field Effect Transistor 场效应晶体管 （FET） 1:15:03

Chapter 5 The field effect transistor 40 ~ 56

Introduction

The FET is a type of transistor that uses an electric field to control the flow of current.

FET是一种类型的晶体管，使用电场来控制电流的流动。

  • FETs are unipolar devices with three terminals.

    FET 是具有三个端子的单极器件

  • FETs control the flow of current by the application of a voltage to the gate terminal, thereby altering the conductivity between the drain and source

    FET 通过向栅极端子gate terminal施加电压来控制电流的流动，从而改变漏极drain和源极source间的导电性

The concept of a field-effect transistor （FET） was first patented by Julius Edgar Lilienfeld in 1925 and by Oskar Heil in 1934, but they were unable to build a working practical semiconducting device. The first FET device to be successfully built was the junction field-effect transistor in 1945.

The most important applications are integrated circuits （for switching）.

最重要的应用是集成电路（用于开关）。

Thanks to their high input impendence, they are often preferred in complex systems.

由于它们的高输入阻抗，它们在复杂系统中通常是首选。

Basic structure & symbols 基本结构和符号 1:18:19

Two different kind of field effect transistors exist

存在两种不同类型的场效应晶体管：

  1. The metal-oxide-silicon field effect transistor = MOSFET.

    The name indicates the structure of the field effect transistor.

  2. The junction field effect transistor = JFET

MOSFET

  • N-channel enhancement MOSFET
  • N-channel depletion MOSFET
  • P-channel enhancement MOSFET
  • P-channel depletion MOSFET

A MOSFET is a component with three external leads:

  1. Gate （G）
  2. Drain （D）
  3. Source （S）

In between the Drain and the Source a channel may exist that can conduct current.

在漏极Drain和源极Source之间可能存在可以传导电流的通道。

  • When this channel consists of negative free carriers, we call it an n-channel MOSFET.
  • When it consists of positive carriers, it is called a p-channel MOSFET.

The direction of the arrow in the MOSFET symbol indicates the type of channel:

  • for an n-channel, the arrow points from Drain to Source
  • for a p-channel the arrow points from Source to Drain

The possible existence of a channel is controlled by the voltage applied over the Gate

通道的可能存在由施加在栅极上的电压控制：

  • When a channel exists without a Gate voltage, this is called a depletion MOSFET.
    • the channel is indicated as a full line
  • When a channel can’t exist without a Gate voltage, this is called an enhancement MOSFET.
    • the channel is indicated with a dotted line

Because the MOSFET has three external leads, we can choose two independent currents and two independent voltages to characterize the MOSFET.

  • For the independent currents we choose the Gate current Ig and the Drain current Id.
  • For alternating currents with low frequencies, the Gate current will be equal to zero, Ig=0. Taken the law of Kirchhoff into account, this means that the current flow out of the Source will be equal to the Drain current Id. The independent voltages are the Gate-Source Vgs and the Drain-Source Vds. Following the law of Kirchhoff, this means the voltage between the Gate and Drain will be Vds-Vgs.

MOSFET: structure

N-channel enhancement MOSFET

The p-layer exists of a semiconductor, lightly doped with positive free carriers.

p-层存在半导体，轻掺杂有正自由载流子。

In this layer two regions are added with a high doping level of negative carriers, n+.

在该层中，两个区域添加了高掺杂水平的负载流子 n+。

This two n+ regions are connected to the Drain and Source leads.

这两个 n+ 区域连接到漏极和源极引线。

Between this connections of the Drain and Source, an oxide is applied, which behaves as an insulator.

在漏极和源极的这种连接之间，施加了一种氧化物，其作用相当于绝缘体。

On top of the oxide a metal strip is applied, which is the Gate.

在氧化物顶部应用金属条，即栅极。

Below this Gate there is the metal-oxide-silicon structure of the MOSFET.

在该栅极下方是 MOSFET 的金属氧化物硅结构。

N-channel depletion MOSFET

该结构类似于 n 沟道增强型 MOSFET，但现在在两个 n+区域之间存在 n 沟道，即使当Vgs=0 时也是如此。 这称为耗尽型 MOSFET。 为了去除沟道，必须将Vgs降低到零以下，Vgs < 0，这将排斥氧化物下方的自由载流子。

P-channel enhancement MOSFET

与 n 沟道增强型 MOSFET 相比，p 型和 n 型半导体的作用发生了转换，负和正自由载流子的作用也是如此。

当 Vgs=0 时，两个 p+区之间没有导电沟道，称为 Drain 和 Source。 这称为增强型 MOSFET。

当 Vgs<0 时，正载流子被吸引到氧化物上。 这些将形成可以在两个 p+ 区之间传导载流子的 p 沟道。 这称为 p 沟道 MOSFET。

P-channel depletion MOSFET

该结构很像 p 沟道增强型 MOSFET，但现在当 Vgs=0 时已经存在 p 沟道。 这称为耗尽型 MOSFET。 为了去除p沟道，必须使Vgs>0，这样正载流子从氧化物下方被排出。

Characteristics of a FET 特性

Input characteristics 输入特性 1:23:30

The connection between Gate and Source is called the input of the field effect transistor.

栅极和源极之间的连接称为场效应晶体管的输入端。

The input characteristic gives the relation between Ig and Vgs. Connected to the Gate is always an insulator:

  • with the MOSFET this is an oxide
  • with the JFET this is a junction diode with a negative voltage.

This means there is never a current flow through the input of the field effect transistor, Ig=0.

这意味着永远不会有电流流过场效应晶体管的输入端，Ig=0

General output characteristics 输出特性

The connection between Drain and Source is considered to be the output of the field effect transistor.

Drain 和 Source 之间的连接被认为是场效应晶体管的输出。

The output characteristics gives the relation between Id and Vds. This relationship is in function of the input voltage Vgs.

The general output characteristic of a field effect transistor is visualised. One solid line represents the output characteristic for one specific input voltage Vgs.

For an n-channel transistor following conditions apply:

对于 n 沟道晶体管，以下条件适用：

  • Close to the origin is a linear relation between the output current Id and the output voltage Vds.

    接近原点的是输出电流Id和输出电压Vds之间的线性关系。

    • This indicates that the output of the field effect transistor behaves as a resistor.

      这表明场效应晶体管的输出充当电阻器。

  • The slope of this linear relation is the resistor value and is dependent on the input voltage Vgs.

    这种线性关系的斜率是电阻值，取决于输入电压 Vgs。

  • Now one can see that the output resistor is adjustable through the input voltage: Vds/Id=R（Vgs）.

    现在可以看到输出电阻可通过输入电压进行调节：Vds/Id=R（Vgs）。

    • The bigger the input voltage Vgs, the more free carriers are present in the n-channel, the lower the resistance R（Vgs） of the channel.

      输入电压Vgs越大，n 沟道中存在的自由载流子越多，沟道的电阻 R（Vgs）越低。

Output current

  • The output current Id is function of the output voltage Vds, as long as Vds < Vgs-Vt.

    • The threshold voltage Vt is the minimal voltage the input voltage Vgs should have to have a channel between Drain and Source.

      阈值电压 Vt 是输入电压 Vgs 在漏极和源极之间应具有通道的最小电压。

    • For a depletion n-channel field effect transistor: Vt<0

      对于耗尽型 n 沟道场效应晶体管：Vt <0

  • When Vds>Vgs-Vt, the output current will be independent of the output voltage Vds （horizontal part of the characteristic）.

    当 Vds>Vgs-Vt 时，输出电流将与输出电压 Vds 无关 （特征的水平部分）。

    • This indicates that the output of the transistor behaves as a current source.

      这表明晶体管的输出充当电流源

The amount of current the current source delivers is dependent on the input voltage Vgs, this is called a voltage induced current source, Id=f（Vgs）.

电流源提供的电流量取决于输入电压Vgs，这称为电压感应电流源，Id =f（Vgs）。

The higher Vgs, the more carriers in the channel, and a bigger current flow between Drain and Source.

Vgs 越高，沟道中的载流子越多，漏极和源极之间的电流越大。

A p-channel transistor behaves in the same way, except from the fact that the signs of Vgs, Vds and Id have to be inverted （see purple signs）.

Current-voltage characteristics 电流-电压特性

The current-voltage characteristics in the three different operation regimes:

Linear region

For an n-channel transistor following conditions apply:

对于 n 沟道晶体管，以下条件适用：

  • Close to the origin is a linear relation between the output current Id and the output voltage Vds.

    接近原点的是输出电流Id和输出电压Vds之间的线性关系。

    • This indicates that the output of the field effect transistor behaves as a resistor.

      这表明场效应晶体管的输出充当电阻器。

  • The slope of this linear relation is the resistor value and is dependent on the input voltage Vgs.

    这种线性关系的斜率是电阻值，取决于输入电压 Vgs。

  • Now one can see that the output resistor is adjustable through the input voltage: Vds/Id=R（Vgs）.

    现在可以看到输出电阻可通过输入电压进行调节：Vds/Id=R（Vgs）。

    • The bigger the input voltage Vgs, the more free carriers are present in the n-channel, the lower the resistance R（Vgs） of the channel.

      输入电压Vgs越大，n 沟道中存在的自由载流子越多，沟道的电阻 R（Vgs）越低。

"Transition" region

The output current Id is function of the output voltage Vds, as long as Vds < Vgs-Vt.

  • The threshold voltage Vt is the minimal voltage the input voltage Vgs should have to have a channel between Drain and Source.

    阈值电压 Vt 是输入电压 Vgs 在漏极和源极之间应具有通道的最小电压。

  • For a depletion n-channel field effect transistor: Vt<0

    对于耗尽型 n 沟道场效应晶体管：Vt <0

It can be shown mathematically that the function is parabolic: \[ I_{d}=k(V_{gs}-V_{t})^{2} \] This characteristic is only valid when the output voltage is big enough:Vds>Vgs-Vt.

该特性仅在输出电压足够大时有效：Vds>Vgs-Vt。

Summary 1:34:25

FET: applications & properties

The behaviour of an n-channel transistor is summarized. This behaviour can also be shown by replacing the transistor by an equivalent circuit

总结了 n 沟道晶体管的行为。这种行为也可以通过等效电路替换晶体管来显示

Equivalent circuits 等效电路

Adjustable resistor 可调电阻

If the output voltage Vds is small, Vds << Vgs-Vt, then we are close to the origin, which means that the transistor behaves as an adjustable resistor. In this case, the transistor can be replaced by the equivalent circuit.

On the input side, the FET behaves as an open circuit, since the input current Ig=0. On the output side, the FET behaves as an adjustable resist or that is controlled by the input voltage Vgs.

在输入端，FET 表现为开路，因为输入电流 Ig=0。 在输出侧，FET 充当可调电阻器或由输入电压 Vgs 控制。

Voltage induced current source 电压感应电流源

If the output voltage Vds is big enough, Vds > Vgs-Vt, then we are on the horizontal part of one of the characteristics.

This means the transistor behaves as a voltage induced current source. In this case the transistor circuit can be replaced by the equivalent circuit.

这意味着晶体管充当电压感应电流源。 在这种情况下，晶体管电路可以用图 中的等效电路代替。

The input current Ig=0 and thus there is an open circuit at the input of the FET. On the output, the FET behaves as a current source, which is a parabolic function of the input voltage Vgs, as shown in equation. \[ I_{d}=k(V_{gs}-V_{t})^{2} \] 输入电流 Ig=0，因此在 FET 的输入端存在开路。 在输出端，FET 充当电流源，它是输入电压 Vgs 的抛物线函数，如公式所示。

Because of this parabolic function, the equivalent circuit is nonlinear.

由于这个抛物线函数，等效电路是非线性的。

To study this nonlinear circuit, we split the voltages and currents in their direct and alternating components.

为了研究这种非线性电路，我们将电压和电流分为直流分量和交流分量。

The time dependent currents and voltages in the equivalent circuit from Figure are split up in a time independent DC component and a time dependent AC component with average value zero.

图中等效电路中与时间相关的电流和电压被分成与时间无关的直流分量和与时间相关的交流分量，其平均值为零。

For the DC components, the nonlinear circuit of Fig is valid. Once the bias point is determined, a linearization is performed on the nonlinear circuit for small AC variations.

对于直流分量，图中的非线性电路是有效的。 一旦确定了偏置点，就会对非线性电路执行线性化，以获得小的 AC 变化。

For this linearization the tangent line to the parabolic characteristic in the bias point is used, instead of the parabolic function itself. For the AC components the equivalent circuit of Figure is valid.

对于这种线性化，使用了偏置点中抛物线特性的切线，而不是抛物线函数本身。 对于交流元件，图中的等效电路是有效的。

参数 gm 是 FET 的电压-电流增益voltage-to-current gain。 它具有导电性或电导的量纲，称为 FET 的跨导transconductance。 该参数的值可以通过公式围绕偏置点的线性化来计算。 \[ g_{m}=\frac{d(k(V_{gs}-V_{t})^{2})}{dV_{gs}}=2k(V_{gs}-V_{t}) \]

CMOS logic gates 逻辑门 1:39:16

From the characteristics of the FET it is clear we can use this element for several applications, such as:

从 FET 的特性来看，很明显我们可以将此元件用于多种应用，例如：

  • An adjustable resistor, an amplifier, a switch, an element with a parabolic function,…

    一个可调电阻器、一个放大器、一个开关、一个具有抛物线函数的元件……

  • a current source 电流源

  • but most important as a logic gate

CMOS NOT or inverter gate

Figure shows the simplest example, a CMOS NOT or inverter gate. The CMOS NOT gate exists of a p-channel enhancement MOSFET and an n-channel enhancement MOSFET in series.

上图显示了最简单的示例，CMOS 非门或反相门。 CMOS 非门存在串联的 p 沟道增强型 MOSFET 和 n 沟道增强型 MOSFET。

This is placed between a supply power Vdd and the ground, 0V. 它位于电源 Vdd 和地之间，0V。

  • The gate voltage of both MOSFETs is the input voltage Vin.

    两个 MOSFET 的栅极电压是输入电压 Vin。

  • The output voltage Vo is measured on the point in between both MOSFETs.

    在两个 MOSFET 之间的点上测得的输出电压 Vo

The MOSFETs behave as open or closed switches when digital input voltages are used.

当使用数字输入电压时，MOSFET 表现为开路或闭合开关。

For a high input voltage Vin:

  • Negative carriers are attracted to the n-channel of the bottom MOSFET.

    负载流子被吸引到底部 MOSFET 的 n 沟道。

  • This MOSFET starts to conduct current and behaves as a closed switch between Drain and Source.

    该 MOSFET 开始传导电流并充当漏极和源极之间的闭合开关。

  • No carriers present in the channel of the top MOSFET, the MOSFET does not conduct current and behaves as an open switch between Drain and Source.

    顶部 MOSFET 的channel 中不存在载流子，MOSFET 不传导电流并且表现为漏极和源极之间的开路开关

The output is connected to ground, thus the output voltage is low.

For a low input voltage Vin:

  • There are no carriers in the n-channel MOSFET.

    n 沟道 MOSFET 中没有载流子

    This MOSFET doesn’t conduct current and acts as an open switch between Drain and Source.

    该 MOSFET 不传导电流并充当漏极和源极之间的开路开关

  • The positive carriers are attracted to the p-channel of the top MOSFET.

    正载流子被吸引到顶部 MOSFET 的 p 沟道。

    This MOSFET conducts current and acts as a closed switch between Drain and Source.

    该 MOSFET 传导电流并充当漏极和源极之间的闭合开关。

CMOS NOR gate

此外，存在具有多个输入的数字门。

A digital NOR = not or gate is defined as the inverse of an OR-gate.

数字 NOR = not or 门被定义为 OR 门的反相。

An OR-gate is a gate where the output is high from the moment one of the inputs has a high voltage.

或门是一种门，其中从输入之一具有高电压的那一刻起，输出就为高电平。

或非门的符号和真值表如图所示。或非门的结构由图中的 CMOS 电路实现。

  1. 第一对p沟道和n沟道MOSFET具有输入电压Via。
  2. 第二对 p 和 n 沟道 MOSFET 具有输入电压 Vib。

p 沟道 MOSFET 串联，n 沟道并联。The p-channel MOSFETS are put in series, the n-channel in parallel.

When at least one of the inputs Via or Vib is high:

  • The analogue n-channel conducts （closed switch） and connects the output Vo with the ground.

    模拟 n 通道导通（闭合开关）并将输出 Vo 与地连接

  • The analogue p-channels does not conduct （open switch）, there is an open circuit between the output Vo and ground

    模拟 p 通道不导通（打开开关），输出 Vo 和地之间存在开路

The output is connected to ground and is low.

When both inputs Via or Vib are low:

  • Both n-channels don't conduct current （open switch）, their parallel circuit is an open circuit between Vo and ground.

    两个 n 通道都不传导电流（打开开关），它们的并联电路是 Vo 和地之间的开路

  • Both p-channels conduct current （closed switch）, the output Vo is connected to the supply voltage through a closed series circuit

  • 两个 p 通道都传导电流（闭合开关），输出 Vo 通过闭合串联电路连接到电源电压

The output will be high.
