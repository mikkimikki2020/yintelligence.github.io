What is a semiconductor? 31:35

能级（英语：Energy level）理论是一种解释原子核外电子运动轨道的一种理论。它认为电子只能在特定的、分立的轨道上运动，各个轨道上的电子具有分立的能量，这些能量值即为能级。电子可以在不同的轨道间发生跃迁，电子吸收能量可以从低能级跃迁到高能级或者从高能级跃迁到低能级从而辐射出光子。氢原子的能级可以由它的光谱显示出来。

  • 1 s orbital and 3 p orbitals: always a bonding and anti-bonding state
  • at 0 K, all electrons in the bonding states
  • bonding s-level is deeply bond, always occupied
  • bonding p-levels
    • = 3 valence bands
    • at higher temperature less occupied.
  • First anti-bonding state = s-type = conduction band

From orbitals to band 39:56

Practically continuous energy levels (conceptually like a liquid)

Bands are composed of closely spaced orbitals

导带（英语：conduction band），又名传导带，是指半导体或是绝缘体材料中，一种电子所具有能量的范围。这个能量的范围高于价带（valence band），而所有在导带中的电子均可经由外在的电场加速而形成电流。

  • 导带 No electrons at 0K, but if electrons are, they can move (current)
  • 价带 Full of electrons at 0K, but if electrons are gone (hole), electrons can move (current)

But what about ...? 57:20

Elementary model is too coarse

  • Why can not all semiconductors emit light?

    When the momentum vectors (k) in a Brillouin zone representing a minimum-energy state in the conduction band and maximum-energy state in the valence band of a semiconductor are different, the material has an indirect gap.

    The energy band gap is called direct if the k-vector is the same in both the conduction band and the valence band. In that case, an electron can emit a photon.

    In an indirect gap, the electron cannot emit a photon because it has to pass through an intermediate state and transfer momentum to the crystal lattice in forms of phonons.

  • Why are not all semiconductors suitable for photovoltaics

Parabolic bands 1:03:13

让我们首先考虑占领的概率。电子是费米子，对于相对于费米能级测量的给定能量E， 它们服从费姆-狄拉克分布函数。

费米能级是绝对零度下电子所能够占据的最高能级，每个能级上面能够放自旋相反的两个电子。

楼梯的比喻不错。设想你有一麻袋苹果（电子），你面前有一个长长的阶梯（能带），你从最下面一级台阶（能级）起往上走，每到一级台阶（能级），就在这一级台阶上面放两个苹果（电子），一直继续下去，到放完为止。此时你站立的台阶，就是费米能级。

我们观察到，在存在带隙的情况下，价带和导带中的可能状态仅与分布的尾部重叠。对于远离带边的能量，分布函数呈指数下降，并且这些状态几乎不存在。

因此，允许忽略精确的能带结构并分别在价带最大值 (VBM) 和导带最小值 (CBM) 附近扩展k 中二阶泰勒级数的能量依赖性。这被称为parabolic band approximation.。

Direct band gap semiconductors 1:08:19

An electron in the conduction band can fall to an empty state in the valence band, giving off the energy difference (Eg) as a photon of light.

Thus, this semiconductor material is used for light emitters and lasers.

Indirect band gap semiconductors 1:09:57

An electron in the conduction band minimum can not fall directly to the valence band maximum.

Instead, it must undergo a momentum change as well as changing its energy. The indirect transition involves a change in k, and the energy is generally given up as heat to the lattice rather than as an emitted photon.

當溫度升高時，有更多的粒子躍遷到傳導帶，因此半導體的傳導性粗略來看會隨著溫度升高而增加。

在固态物理学中，价带和导带是最接近费米能级的带，因此决定了固体的电导率 electrical conductivity。在非金属中，价带是电子能量的最高范围，其中电子通常在绝对零温度下存在，而导带是空电子态的最低范围。在材料的电子能带结构图中，价带位于费米能级下方，而导带位于其上方。

其中k是波尔兹曼常数，T为绝对温度（热力学温标），T=0K时，化学势就是系统的费米能。半导体中电子的费米能，也被被称为费米能级。

Doping (Extrinsic Semiconductors) 1:23:32

Intrinsic semiconductors are semiconductors, which do not contain impurities. They do contain electrons as well as holes.

The electron density equals the hole density since the thermal activation of an electron from the valence band to the conduction band yields a free electron in the conduction band as well as a free hole in the valence band.

本征载流子密度的温度依赖性主要取决于能带隙的指数依赖性。此外，必须考虑有效态密度和能带隙的温度依赖性。

Doped semiconductors are semiconductors, which contain impurities, foreign atoms incorporated into the crystal structure of the semiconductor.

由于在半导体生长过程中缺乏控制，这些杂质可能是无意的，或者它们可以故意添加以在半导体中提供自由载流子。

与被取代的晶格原子相比，取代供体和受体在其外电子壳中具有过量或不足的电子。Substitutional donors and acceptors have an excess or a deficit electron in their outer electron shell as compared to the replaced lattice atom.

Shallow impurities浅层杂质需要很少的能量——通常在热能附近，kT 或更少——来电离。

浅层杂质容易电离，因此自由载流子密度free carrier density等于杂质浓度impurity concentration。

Deep impurities深层杂质需要比热能大得多的能量才能电离，因此只有一小部分存在于半导体中的杂质对自由载流子有贡献。

远离带边缘的热能是其五倍以上的深层杂质极不可能电离。 这些杂质可能是有效的复合中心，电子和空穴在其中坠落并相互湮灭。 如此深的杂质也称为陷阱traps

n-Doped semiconductors 1:25:29

Ionized donors provide free electrons in a semiconductor, which is then called n-type, while ionized acceptors provide free holes in a semiconductor, which we refer to as being a p-type semiconductor.

  • Weakly bound electron
  • Small binding energies -> Easily broken at room temperature
  • Produces an electron in the conduction band

Carrier concentration vs. Temperature 1:31:17

Ionized impurity scattering occurs when there are many donor or acceptor dopants present in the lattice.

In general, scattering decreases electron mobility of charge carriers by acting as obstacles. In other words, charge carriers are not able to flow easily throughout the material.

随着掺杂的增加，每个添加的施主或受主水平都可以被认为是添加到晶格的额外“散射中心”，这是另一个阻碍电荷载流子运动的障碍。这种类型的散射不依赖于任何类型的热振动，而是利用了载流子没有足够的能量移动到任何不同能级的事实。

Carrier Energy barriers are not an issue when thermal energy is a major factor.

This means that for both n and p-type conductors, ionized impurity scattering is dominant at lower temperatures and its effects gradually decrease as the temperature increases.

中间阶段取决于杂质浓度。

Thermal motion of carriers 1:41:07

No net current

  • Thermal equilibrium -> carriers move randomly due to thermal energy

  • Collisions happen with

    • Impurities (lattice irregularities, doping)
    • Lattice vibrations (=phonons)

  • Collisions = inelastic scattering event (Energy loss)

  • Average time between collisions: 𝛕𝒄 (mean collision/scattering time)

  • Thermal motion is completely random -> averages out to zero ->no current

Carrier Drift 1:45:25

Motion of free carriers in a semiconductor caused by an electric field due to an externally applied voltage leads to current. We will refer to this transport mechanism as carrier drift.

Mobility 1:51:39

The electron mobility and hole mobility have a similar doping dependence电子迁移率和空穴迁移率具有相似的掺杂依赖性

  • For low doping concentrations, the mobility is almost constant and is primarily limited by phonon scattering.

    对于低掺杂浓度，迁移率几乎是恒定的，主要受光子散射的限制

  • At higher doping concentrations, the mobility decreases due to ionized impurity scattering with the ionized doping atoms.

    在较高的掺杂浓度下，迁移率会由于电离掺杂原子的电离杂质散射而降低。

Note that the mobility is linked to the total number of ionized impurities or the sum of the donor and acceptor densities. 请注意，迁移率与电离杂质的总数或供体和受体密度的总和有关。

The minority carrier mobility also depends on the total impurity density. 少数载流子迁移率还取决于总杂质密度。

The minority carrier mobility can be approximated by the majority-carrier mobility in a material with the same number of impurities少数载流子迁移率可以通过具有相同数量杂质的材料中的多数载流子迁移率来近似。

Diffusion 1:56:47

Carriers also move from regions where the carrier density is high to regions where the carrier density is low.

This carrier transport mechanism is due to the thermal energy and the associated random motion of the carriers. We will refer to this transport mechanism as carrier diffusion.

Anisotropic carrier densities lead to net current

Carrier diffusion is due to the thermal energy, kBT, which causes the carriers to move at random even when no field is applied. 载流子扩散是由热能 kBT 引起的，即使没有施加场，它也会导致载流子随机移动。

这种随机运动不会产生载体的净流量，也不会产生在具有均匀载流子密度的材料中产生净电流，因为任何离开特定位置的载流子平均都会被另一个载流子取代。

但是，如果存在载流子梯度，则扩散过程会使载流子密度变化变得均匀：载流子从密度高的区域扩散到密度低的区域

Summary 2:00:46

Basics of semiconductors

  • Semiconductors
    • Discrete energy levels + lattice = bands
    • Semiconductors are characterized by a “medium”-sized bandgap
  • Carrier densities
    • Density of states + occupancy probability = carrier distribution
    • Fermi level is the main variable determining free electron & hole quantities.
  • Extrinsic semiconductors
    • Doping can alter the Fermi level to change the free carrier makeup of the semiconductor
    • Core mechanism used for fabrication of active components (n-doping, p-doping)
  • Modelling current
    • Net motion of carriers. Described by diffusion, drift and (indirectly) by thermal motion.
