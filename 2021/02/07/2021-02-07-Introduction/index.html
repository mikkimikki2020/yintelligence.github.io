History

Reading Wheel 阅读轮 (Bookwheel)

  • Described by Agostino Ramelli in 1588

  • Keep several books open to read from them at the same time

    保持多本书打开以同时阅读它们

    • comparable to modern tabbed browsing

      可与现代标签式浏览相媲美

  • The reading wheel has never really been built

  • Could be seen as a predecessor of hypertext

    可以看作是超文本的前身

Dewey Decimal Classification (DDC)

  • Library classification system 图书馆分类系统

    • developed by Melvil Dewey in 1876

  • Hierarchical classification 等级分类

    • 10 main classes with 10 divisions each and 10 sections per division
    • total of 1000 sections

  • After the three numbers, decimals can be used for further sub-classification

    在三个数字之后，可以使用小数进行进一步的子分类

  • Documents can appear in more than one class

    文档可以出现在多个类中

    • however, there is normally only one physical copy (one main class)

      然而，通常只有一个物理副本（一个主类）

  • Different alternatives

    • Library of Congress (LC) classification
    • Universal Decimal Classification (UDC) by Paul Otlet and Henri La Fontaine

"As We May Think" (1945)

Bush's article As We May Think (1945) is often seen as the "origin" of hypertext

Bush 的文章 As We May Think (1945) 通常被视为超文本的“起源”

When data of any sort are placed in storage, they are filed alphabetically or numerically, and information is found (when it is) by tracing it down from subclass to subclass.

当任何类型的数据被放入存储中时，它们会按字母或数字排列，并通过从一个子类到另一个子类进行追踪来找到信息（如果是的话）。

It can be in only one place, unless duplicates are used; one has to have rules as to which path will locate it, and the rules are cumbersome.

它只能在一个地方，除非使用重复项；必须有关于哪条路径将定位它的规则，并且规则是繁琐的。

Having found one item, moreover, one has to emerge from the system and re-enter on a new path.

此外，找到一个项目后，必须从系统中出现并重新进入新的路径。

The human mind does not work that way. It operates by association... It affords an immediate step, however, to associative indexing, the basic idea of which is a provision whereby any item may be caused at will to select immediately and automatically another. This is the essential feature of the memex. The process of tying two items together is the important thing. ..

人类的思维不是这样运作的。它通过关联运行……然而，它提供了一个直接的步骤，即关联索引，其基本思想是一个规定，可以随意使任何项目立即自动选择另一个项目。这是memex的基本特征。将两个项目联系在一起的过程是重要的事情。..

The article introduces the Memex

  • memory extender

  • store and access information 存储和访问信息

  • follow cross-references in the form of associative trails between pieces of information (microfilms)

    以信息片段（缩微胶片）之间的关联轨迹的形式遵循交叉引用

  • prototypical hypertext machine 原型超文本机

  • trail blazers are those who find delight in the task of establishing useful trails

    开拓者 trail blazers是那些在建立有用的路径的任务中找到乐趣的人

We notice that older classification methods (e.g. Dewey Decimal Classification, or boxes of punch cards) work hierarchically.

我们注意到旧的分类方法（例如杜威十进制分类或穿孔卡片盒）是分层工作的。

One of the problems there is that content needs to be physically duplicated in order to be in multiple places at the same time (e.g. a book can only be in one library section at the same time).

存在的问题之一是内容需要物理复制才能同时位于多个位置（例如，一本书只能同时位于图书馆的一个部分）。

The Memex differs from this in the way that it works by association, just like the human memory. Content is associated with each other, and content can be associated with many other items without duplication.

Memex 与此的不同之处在于它通过关联工作的方式，就像人类记忆一样。内容彼此关联，并且内容可以与许多其他项目关联而不会重复。

Hypertext 超文本 (1965)

  • Ted Nelson coined the term hypertext
  • Nelson started Project Xanadu in 1960

    • first hypertext project

    • non-sequential writing 无序写作

    • referencing/embedding parts of a document in another document (transclusion) → trans-pointing windows Mockup (1972)

      引用/嵌入文档的各部分中的另一个文档（transclusion）→反式指向窗口样机（1972）

    • bidirectional (bivisible) links

      双向（bivisible）链接

    • version and rights management

      版本和权限管理

  • XanaduSpace 1.0 was released as part of Project Xanadu in 2007
  • OpenXanadu demo/deliverable released in 2014

Digital Documents as a Paper Simulator? 数字文档作为纸张模拟器？

Most people don't understand the logic of the concept: "What You See Is What You Get" is based on printing the document out ("get" means "get WHEN YOU PRINT IT OUT").

大多数人不理解这个概念的逻辑：“所见即所得”是基于将文档打印出来（“get”的意思是“当你打印出来时得到”）。

And that means a metaphysical shift: a document can only consist of what can be printed! This re-froze the computer document into a closed rectangular object which cannot be penetrated by outside markings (curtailing what you could do with paper).

这意味着形而上学的转变：文档只能由可以打印的内容组成！这将计算机文档重新冻结成一个封闭的矩形对象，外部标记无法穿透该对象（减少了您可以用纸做的事情）。

No marginal notes, no sticky notes, no crossouts, no insertions, no overlays, no highlighting - PAPER UNDER GLASS.

没有边注，没有便利贴，没有划线，没有插入，没有覆盖，没有突出显示 - 玻璃下的纸

Hypertext Editing System 超文本编辑系统 HES (1967)

  • Early hypertext system 早期的超文本系统
    • developed at Brown University (1967) by Andries van Dam and his team
    • Ted Nelson was a visitor at Brown University a that time
  • Limitations
    • unidirectional links 单向链接
    • non-overlapping links 非重叠链接
    • only embedded links 仅嵌入链接
  • File Retrieval and Editing System 文件检索和编辑系统, FRESS (1968)

    • follow-up project taking ideas from HES and NLS

    • first system introducing 'undo' functionality

      第一个引入“撤销undo”功能的系统

oNLine System 在线系统 (NLS) - The Mother of All Demos (1968)

  • Douglas Engelbart and his colleagues at the Stanford Research Institute developed the oNLine System (NLS) as part of the Augment Project
    • vision about the future of interactive computing
  • NLS was demonstrated at the Fall Joint Computer Conference in 1968

    • showed first practical use of hypertext

      展示了超文本的第一个实际用途

    • computer mouse 电脑鼠标

    • remote collaboration (connected computers)

      远程协作（连接的计算机）

    • raster-scan video monitors

      光栅扫描视频监视器

    • screen windows

      屏幕窗口

    • ...

Aspen Movie map (1978)

  • Early hypermedia system 早期的超媒体系统

    • developed at MIT by Andrew Lippman and his team

    • hypermedia = extension of hypertext with other media types (e.g. images, sounds)

      超媒体= 用其他媒体类型（例如图像、声音）扩展超文本

  • Virtual tour of Aspen

    • pictures taken every 10 feet while driving through the city

      开车穿过城市时每 10 英尺拍摄一次照片

    • additional linked media (e.g. images and sounds)

      附加链接媒体（例如图像和声音）

  • Similar concept now used in Google Street View

HyperCard (1987)

  • One of the early widespread hypermedia systems

  • Released by Apple Computer Inc. (as part of System Software 6)

    • developed by Bill Atkinson

  • Information is stored in a series of cards that are arranged into stacks

    信息存储在排列成堆栈的一系列卡片中

  • Links can be defined between different cards

    不同卡片之间可以定义链接

  • HyperCards may contain text, pictures, audio and video

    HyperCards 可能包含文本、图片、音频和视频

    • HyperTalk programming language is used to execute commands and jump to other cards

      HyperTalk 编程语言用于执行命令和跳转到其他卡

ARPANET (1969)

Advanced Research Projects Agency Network

  • created by DARPA (US Department of Defense)

  • first operational packet switching network

    第一个可操作的分组交换网络

  • first ARPANET link established in November 1969

ARPANET applications

  • Email (1971), Ray Tomlinson
  • FTP (1973)

ARPANET Map (March 1977)

TCP (1974)

  • Transmission control protocol 传输控制协议

    • replacement of Network Control Protocol (NCP)

      替代网络控制协议 (NCP)

  • 'A Protocol for Packet Network Interconnection'

    • by Vint Cerf and Bob Kahn

  • Reliable and ordered transmission of byte stream between two endpoints

    两个端点之间可靠有序的字节流传输

  • Migration of ARPANET to TCP/IP in 1982

TCP/IP (1978)

  • 4 abstraction layers 抽象层

    • each layer offers functionality to the above layer

      每一层都为上一层提供功能

    • separation of concerns

      关注点分离

  • Application layer 应用层

    • HTTP, FTP, POP, ...

  • Transport layer 传输层

    • TCP, UDP, ...

  • Internet layer 互联网层

    • addressing hosts and packet routing
    • IP, ...

  • Link layer 链路层

World Wide Web 万维网 (WWW)

Networked hypertext system (over ARPANET) to share information at CERN

用于在 CERN 共享信息的联网超文本系统（通过 ARPANET）

  • first draft in March 1989
  • The Information Mine, Information Mesh, …?

Components by end of 1990

  • HyperText Transfer Protocol 超文本传输协议 (HTTP)
  • HyperText Markup Language 超文本标记语言 (HTML)
  • HTTP server software
  • Web browser called World Wide Web

First public "release" in August 1991

WordWideWeb Browser (1993)

WWW and Hypertext 万维网和超文本

WWW is mainly a network-enabled version of the HES hypertext model

WWW主要是HES超文本模型的网络版本

  • unidirectional links between heterogeneous resources

    异构资源之间的单向链接

  • is it more than just a digital version of paper documents with links?

What about all the richer functionality researched by the hypertext community?

  • bidirectional links
  • transclusion and external (non-embedded) links
  • version management
  • …

Is there something wrong with the WWW?

Mobile Web 移动网络

  • New forms of connectivity and information exchange

    新形式的连接和信息交流

    • P2P networks

  • New requirements and functionality

    • location-based services 基于位置的服务
    • voice navigation 语音导航

  • Access the Web from anywhere at anytime

  • 随时随地访问网络

Web 2.0

User becomes an author and shares information

用户成为作者并分享信息

  • tagging 标记
  • Wikis 维基
  • social networking 社交网络
  • mashups 混搭
  • ...

Not a new technology!

Why did some of these things not happen earlier?

  • limitations of the original World Wide Web?

Semantic Web (Web 3.0)

  • Add explicit semantics to web resources

    向 Web 资源添加显式语义

  • Machine-interpretable Web

    机器可解释的网络

  • Use of ontologies

    本体的使用

  • Potential reasoning over Web resources

    对网络资源的潜在推理

Internet of Things / Web of Things 物联网/物联网

  • Mark Weiser coined the term Ubiquitous Computing 无处不在计算 while working at Xerox PARC

    • M. Weiser, The Computer for the 21st Century, ACM Mobile Computing and Communications Review, July 1999

  • Related terms are Disappearing Computing, Pervasive Computing or Internet of Things

    相关术语是消失的计算、普适计算或物联网

  • Physical objects with embedded computing functionality that actively or passively participate in the Web

    主动或被动参与 Web 的具有嵌入式计算功能的物理对象

    • mobile phones, RFID-tagged objects, smart pens, …

  • Do we have to extend the current web infrastructure?
