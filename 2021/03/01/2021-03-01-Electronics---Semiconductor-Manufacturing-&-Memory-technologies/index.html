Silicon 硅 → Wafers 晶圆 3:41

From silicon ore to silicon wafers 从硅矿石到硅片

Reduction of quartzite (SiO2) 石英岩的还原 5:38

Submerged electrode arc furnace

Reduction to ~98% pure Si (mostly Al & Fe impurities)

SiC(s) + SiO2(s) → Si(l) + SiO(g) + CO(g)

Purification of silicon 硅的提纯 8:40

Metallurgical grade Si → Electronic grade Si

  • Fractional distillation process:

    Si(s) +3HCl(g) → SiHCl3(g) +H2(g) +heat

  • Fluidized bed reactor @300°C:

    SiHCl3(g) +H2(g) → Si(s) +3HCl(g)

  • Can be looped to increase purity to less than 1 part per 109.

Making polycrystalline Si into a monocrystalline Si 将多晶硅制成单晶硅 11:44

Starting from a seed crystal

Main used technique: Czochralski crystal growth method 直拉晶体生长法 (>1412°C)

  • Suited for large Si wafers but may have some oxygen inclusion impurities.

Starting from a seed crystal

Alternative technique: Float zone technique 浮动区域技术

Very high purity but small wafers. Used for detectors, power devices, expensive high-efficiency solar panels (spacecraft). Also, highly transparent to THz waves.

纯度非常高，但晶圆很小。用于探测器、功率器件、昂贵的高效太阳能电池板（航天器）。此外，对T赫兹波高度透明。

Wafer shaping & cutting 晶圆成型和切割 18:06

Largest wafers used today: 300mm diameter, ~775µm thick

  • Remove both ends of ingot
  • Surface grinding, edge bevelling
  • Wafer cutting with industrial grade diamond.

Wafers 晶圆 → Integrated circuits 集成电路

Clean rooms 洁净室 21:41

Design & contamination control

  • Modern IC chips have transistors in the 10nm range → sub-µm particles may damage or destroy parts of chips → cleanroom to minimize contaminants

    现代 IC 芯片具有 10 纳米范围内的晶体管 → 亚微米颗粒可能会损坏或破坏芯片的部分 → 洁净室以最大限度地减少污染物

  • More contaminants means lower wafer yield, performance (lifetime) and reliability

    更多的污染物意味着更低的晶圆产量、性能（寿命）和可靠性

  • ISO cleanroom standards: maximum number of particles/volume bigger than some threshold

    ISO 洁净室标准：最大颗粒数/体积大于某个阈值

Contaminant Examples Source
Particles Dust, hair, flakes (µm range) Humans, equipment (friction), chemicals
Airborne contaminants wide range of gaseous contaminants (sulfate, nitrate, organics, ...) Wafer transfer, chemical delivery system ... Wafer transfer, chemical delivery system ...
Bacteria All kind Humans, improperly disinfected materials and equipment
Chemicals Chlorine Lithography, etching, cleaning products, ...
Metal ions Sodium (ppm – ppb range) Electrically active impurities in wafers from processed silicon

Main categories of IC device manufacturing IC器件制造的主要类别 27:53

Layering分层: growing and deposition 生长和沉积

Growing techniques 生长技术 29:49

Consumes underlying silicon for forming new layer

Oxidation: Si + O2 → SiO2

Nitration: 3Si + 2N2 → Si3N4

  • Electrical isolation (oxide layer is superior)
  • Oxide layer acts as the active gate in MOSFET
  • Protective layer, passivation (nitride is superior), T resistance
  • Used for patterning
  • Variants: “wet” and “dry” oxidation

Deposition techniques 沉积技术 34:28

Does not consume silicon, new layer is deposited

  • homo/hetero epitaxy
  • Silicon films: epitaxy layer, polysilicon
  • Dielectric Films: SiO2, Nitride, …
  • Metals: aluminum, tungsten, TiN, …
  • Used for Ga & As
  • Very precise composition can be obtained
  • Can be used for doping too
  • Used for quantum nanostructures

Depositing of thin metallic layers for

  • Contact metals in thin-film transistors
  • Hard disk production
  • Antireflection coating in optics
  • Inexpensive equipment → relatively cheap
  • Relatively uniform and compact deposits, but not as much as CVD or sputtering
  • Generally use for layers > 1µm

Lithography & Etching 光刻和蚀刻

Patterning or Lithography 图案化或光刻 44:41

Selectively mask or expose parts of the wafer

  • Determines the critical dimensions of the device (now 10nm & 7nm)

    确定器件的关键尺寸（现在为 10nm 和 7nm）

  • Highly defect sensitive (pattern creation & alignment)

    对缺陷高度敏感（图案创建和对齐）

  • Reticle: hard copy of design → made with laser writing or electron beam

    标线：设计的硬拷贝→用激光书写或电子束制作

  • Developing-expose through mask; first phase is reversible

    显影-通过掩模曝光；第一阶段是可逆的

  • Photoresists, positive & negative → solubility

    光刻胶，正片和负片 → 溶解度

Etching: removal of top layers from the wafer 蚀刻：从晶圆上去除顶层 51:11

Typically in conjunction with lithography

  • Wet etching: wafers are immersed in tank of etchant (e.g. KOH)
  • Dry etching: exposure to ion bombardment for dislodging surface material

Doping: making extrinsic semiconductors 制造外在半导体 55:11

Achieving specific dopant concentration & distribution, creating junctions

  • Thermal diffusion (impurity source can be solid, liquid or gaseous)
    • High T: lithography can’t be used → SiO2 layer
    • Lateral spread within the material; solubility limit
  • Ion implantation
    • Around room T, conventional lithography possible
    • Leads to damage of wafer surface → post annealing

Packaging 包装 1:00:30

Readying the IC for use

System on a Chip (SoC): all components integrated into one chip

System on a Package (SoP): all components integrated into one chip (e.g., In mobile phones)

Functions of packaging:

  1. Lead system
  2. Physical protection
  3. Minimizing environmental impact
  4. Heat dissipation

Handling interconnects

  • Wirebond

  • Flip-Chip

  • Chip-Stack

Example 示例: MOSFET fabrication steps 1:04:50

NMOS

  1. Lightly P-doped substrate
  2. Oxidation (thick field oxide)
  3. Lithography + oxide etching
  4. Thin, high-quality oxide layer for FET gate formation
  5. Polysilicon deposition (gate electrode material + interconnect medium in IC)
  6. Polysilicon etching
  7. Oxide etching h) High concentration n-doping
  8. Insulating oxide layer
  9. Etching step
  10. Metallization layer (Al) for interconnects
