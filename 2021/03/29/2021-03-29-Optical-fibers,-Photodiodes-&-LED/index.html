Step -index vs. Graded -index fibers

  • Step -index fiber

    • Uniform cladding refractive index
    • More signal distortion, pulse broadening â†’ lower bandwidth
    • More attenuation
    • Higher NA
    • Less expensive

  • Graded -index fiber

    • Variable cladding refractive index
    • Low signal distortion, rays propagate at similar speeds â†’ higher bandwidth
    • Less attenuation
    • Lower NA
    • More expensive

Properties:

  • chromatic dispersion: frequency-dependent phase velocities.
